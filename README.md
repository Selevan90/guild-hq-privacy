 # Guild HQ Privacy Policy                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                            
  _Last updated: 8 May 2026_                                                                                                                                                                                   
  
  This privacy policy explains what data the Guild HQ app ("the App") collects,                                                                                                                                
  how it's used, and your rights.                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## Who we are                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  Guild HQ is an independent World of Warcraft companion app built by                                                                                                                                          
  [YOUR NAME] ("we", "us"). Contact: [YOUR EMAIL].
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## What data we collect                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  The App fetches publicly available game data and a small number of                                                                                                                                           
  locally-stored credentials:
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  - **Public guild and character data** from Blizzard's official Battle.net                                                                                                                                    
    API — character names, classes, item levels, raid progression, Mythic+
    scores. This is the same data Blizzard publishes on their Armory.                                                                                                                                          
  - **Discord server ID and Raid-Helper API key** — only if you choose to                                                                                                                                      
    connect your guild's Raid-Helper schedule. Stored on your device only;                                                                                                                                     
    never transmitted to us.                                                                                                                                                                                   
  - **Public raid log data** from Warcraft Logs, used to identify your                                                                                                                                         
    guild's raid team.                                                                                                                                                                                         
  - **Advertising identifier** — your device's IDFA (iOS) or AAID (Android)
    if you grant tracking permission, used by Google AdMob to serve ads.                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  The App does not collect your name, email, account credentials, or any                                                                                                                                       
  personal information you don't explicitly enter.                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## How we use the data                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  - To display your guild roster, character details, raid schedule, and                                                                                                                                        
    parses inside the App
  - To serve ads via Google AdMob, which keeps the App free                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## Third parties                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  The App communicates with the following services. Data sent to each is                                                                                                                                       
  limited to what's necessary for the feature:
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  - **Blizzard Battle.net API** — public guild / character lookups
  - **Raider.io** — Mythic+ leaderboard data                                                                                                                                                                   
  - **Warcraft Logs** — raid parse data                                                                                                                                                                        
  - **Raid-Helper** — your Discord server's event schedule                                                                                                                                                     
  - **Google AdMob** — ad serving and measurement.                                                                                                                                                             
    See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## Data retention                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  The App caches API responses locally on your device for short periods                                                                                                                                        
  (minutes to hours) to keep performance fast. Uninstalling the App
  removes all cached data. We do not maintain any server-side database                                                                                                                                         
  of your activity.                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA)                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                            
  You have the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold about                                                                                                                                          
  you. Because we do not collect personal data on a server, the practical
  way to exercise this is to clear or uninstall the App.                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  You can also:                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  - Opt out of personalised ads via your device settings                                                                                                                                                       
    (Settings → Privacy → Tracking on iOS, Settings → Google → Ads on Android)
  - Withdraw GDPR consent at any time by tapping "Privacy options" in                                                                                                                                          
    the App's Settings tab _(coming soon)_, which re-shows the consent dialog                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## Children's privacy                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly                                                                                                                                         
  collect data from them.

  If we update this policy we'll change the "Last updated" date at the                                                                                                                                         
  top. Material changes will be flagged in the App.
